Welcome!

The D&D ToolKit was designed with the idea to help players more efficiently manage aspects of their D&D campaigns to help save time and paper!

While not meant to entirely replace manually keeping track of your campaign and characters, it is meant to help with tasks that may be repetitive and require a lot of erasing and rewriting of information.

Some of the features are still being worked on but you can login to test out the Initiative Tracker feature.
